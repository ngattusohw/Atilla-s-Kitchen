Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of fresh salmon
  • 1 to 2 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to medium high or oven to 375 degrees F if baking.
  2. Place salmon on a sheet of aluminum foil (if baking, place in oven-proof dish), drizzle with oil, sprinkle with dill, and salt and pepper.
  3. Place on grill. Leave foil open. You can fold up ends of foil to eliminate oil dripping into grill. If placing in the oven, leave uncovered.
  4. Cook 12 to 15 minutes or until salmon easily flakes with fork. Remove from grill (or oven) and serve immediately.
